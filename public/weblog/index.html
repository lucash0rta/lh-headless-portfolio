Minimising use of AI

Monday 23/3/26

I've come to some shifts in thinking recently. I worked as a vibe coder recently, roughly between the months of September and February, I started to feel like my mind was deteriorating, working within and developing tooling for systems in languages I didn't understand fully, an understanding of the architecture and interoperability yes. But the code? No

I go into my year of research as an honours student at RMIT aiming to minimise my use of AI across the research I conduct and the outputs I create, my stance is that participation with use of online LLM's such as chatGPT and Claude, actively aids the capitalist extractivist class and actively harms us as end users and as the proletariat, in an age where these companies tools are being used in defense, surveillance, datamining, training and targeted ad optimisation. I cannot stand by and be complicit.
